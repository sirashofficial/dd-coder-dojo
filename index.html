About Code with Ubuntu

Code with Ubuntu (formerly Blue Roof CoderDojo) is a CoderDojo club located in Austerville, Durban, and part of the global CoderDojo movement. We provide free coding education to young people aged 7-17.

Our mission is to empower the next generation with digital skills, creative thinking, and problem-solving abilities that will serve them throughout their lives. The rebrand to Code with Ubuntu beautifully captures our philosophy—Ubuntu meaning "I am because we are"—emphasizing the collaborative, community-centered approach that drives our growth and impact.
