About DD Coder Dojo

Located in Austerville, Durban, DD (Digital Democracy) Coder Dojo is part of the global CoderDojo movement, providing free coding education to young people aged 7-17.

Our mission is to empower the next generation with digital skills, creative thinking, and problem-solving abilities that will serve them throughout their lives.
